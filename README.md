# Botanism

Botanism is a Vintage Story mod about believable propagation and cultivation of wild plants, herbs, and flowers.

The goal is to make wild plants renewable through intentional player action, without turning them into a simple resource printer. Plants should be propagated through methods that make sense for them, such as seeds, bulbs, spores, rhizomes, pips, cuttings, or divisions.

## Status

Botanism is currently in early development.

The repository may contain unfinished code, placeholder content, test assets, broken features, or systems that do not make much sense yet. Things may change heavily before the first proper release.

## Planned direction

- Propagation of vanilla wild plants and flowers
- Plant-specific propagation materials
- Believable extraction from wild plants
- Data-driven plant profiles
- Configurable growth and yield behavior
- Compatibility-friendly structure for future plant mods

## Not planned for Botanism

Botanism is focused on intentional cultivation.

Wild ecological regrowth is planned as a separate future mod called Seedwind.
